Pricing

No hidden fees. No set up fees. No monthly or annual feeds. No risk.

Free

For organizations choosing to pass fees on to patrons.

45¢* per ticket + 3.5%

Tickets sold online

10¢* per ticket

Comp, Reserved and Bulk Ticket orders

Call Center

FREE for organizations
$2.50 per order paid by Patrons

*45 ¢ rate applies to shows licensed through Musical Theatre International.
All other shows, per ticket fee is 50¢ + 3.5%, just 5¢ more.

Receive Money Receive your funds via check or e-transfer within 5 business days of the closing of your event. ShowTix4U allows you to request one early payout per event.

FREE for Organizations All fees are passed on to the patron unless you choose to incur fees.

Percentage fee 3.5% only charged on credit card orders.

In-Person Credit Card Sales For in-person transactions at your event/box office, the 45¢ can be passed on to the customer, but the 3.5% is paid by the organization. This is only for in-person credit card transactions and not patron online purchases.

Mail Tickets ShowTIx4U can print and mail tickets to patrons, if they choose, during the purchase process. Patrons will be charged $1.75 per order for this service. There are no fees for you.

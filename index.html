Receive your Funds

When your event ends, download your reports, and receive your funds via check or e-transfer within 5 business days. It’s that easy!

Any fees are subtracted from the balance in your ShowTix4U Account, which is revenue generated from online credit card ticket sales. ShowTix4U allows you to request one early payout per event.
